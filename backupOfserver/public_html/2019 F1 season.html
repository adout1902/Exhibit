2019 F1 season
Lewis Hamilton won the World Drivers' Championpship with 413 points.
His teammate Valtteri Bottas earned second position with 326 points. Overall, he failed to challenge Hamilton significantly.
2019 marked another year of dominance for Mercedes, with their 6th Constructor's Championpship.
George Russell out-qualified his teammate at every race in the 2019 season.
Robert Kubica made his return to F1 in the 2019 season after suffering a major injury in a rally race crash in 2011.
The former world championship-level team were only able to secure one point in the 2019 season after a poor start to pre-season testing.
